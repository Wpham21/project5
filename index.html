My Top EDM DJs

Crankdat

Information about this artist:

An American DJ well known for his bass and dubstep music. He is also known to have a really unique style of performing bass music as some of the music he is produce, featuring sounds from different objects like gun noises or even the sound of the iconic taco bell ring.

John Summit

Information about this artist:

An American DJ and electronic music producer. He is known for his energetic and bass driven style of music, which is a blend of many different electronic music genre like progressive house. A very big name in the house music genre and has performed at many different festivals such as Coachella and Lollapalooza.

Subtronics

Information about this artist:

An American dubstep DJ known for his electronic music. His name is the combination of Subwoofers and electronics and is known to host many music festivals like the one that was recently hosted at the Tacoma Dome in Seattle. (November 2024).
